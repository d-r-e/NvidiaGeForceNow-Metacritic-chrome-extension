Privacy Policy

GeForce NOW Metacritic Score

This extension displays Metacritic score badges on the GeForce NOW games catalog.

Last updated: April 7, 2026

No personal data collection The extension does not collect, sell, or transmit personal data to the developer.
Local cache only Resolved title matches and scores are stored locally in your browser to reduce repeated lookups.

What the extension accesses

  • https://play.geforcenow.com/* to read visible game titles and place score badges in the page UI.
  • https://www.metacritic.com/* to request Metacritic search results for those game titles.
  • chrome.storage.local to cache resolved matches and scores on your device.

What information is stored

The extension stores a local cache of normalized game titles, matched Metacritic titles, score values, related search paths, and cache timestamps in your browser. This cache stays on your device and is used only so the extension does not need to repeat the same lookup on every visit.

What the extension does not do

  • It does not collect account credentials, payment details, or personal profile information.
  • It does not send analytics, telemetry, or advertising identifiers to the developer.
  • It does not track browsing activity outside the declared host permissions.
  • It does not create remote user profiles or sell data to third parties.

Third-party requests

When the extension looks up a game score, it makes a request to Metacritic search pages for the game title. Those requests are necessary for the extension to find a matching score. Your use of GeForce NOW and Metacritic remains subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

If the extension's data practices change, this page will be updated before or at the same time as the related extension release.
